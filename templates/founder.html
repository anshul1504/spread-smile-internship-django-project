OUR FOUNDER

aditya raj singh Chauhan

co-founder & Executive Trustee

Aditya Chauhan is the Co-Founder & Executive Trustee of Smile Foundation, a national level development organization that was established in 2002 by him and his friends as a part of their giving back to the society. Aditya is an alumnus of Indian Institute of Management – Ahmedabad, an Associate Member of Institute of Company Secretaries of India, and a qualified lawyer. Smile Foundation works on the subjects of education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment through 185 projects operational across 25 states in India. He comes from a background where volunteerism comes naturally from school days. So doing social good in one or other way always remain in the back of the mind. At present, Smile Foundation has 74 Mission Education projects functional across India. Aditya is the producer of Feature Film “I am Kalam”, which has achieved astounding success with the film travelling to over 30 countries and winning 22 national and international awards. With “I am Kalam” Smile Foundation advocated the cause of Sending Every Child to School. In an exclusive interview with Rusen Kumar, Founder & Editor-INDIACSR, Aditya Chauhan, Co-Founder Executive Trustee, Smile Foudnation shared about his work and passion towards Social Venture Philanthropy (SVP) model.
